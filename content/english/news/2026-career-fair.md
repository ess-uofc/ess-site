The ESS Winter Career Fair connects engineering students with industry professionals and employers from a wide range of fields. It’s an opportunity to explore career paths, learn about internships and full-time roles, and build meaningful connections that can shape your professional journey. Whether you’re just starting out or preparing to graduate, the career fair offers valuable insights and networking opportunities for students at all stages.
