ESS members get access to events hosted by the society along with many other perks

About our Membership

Support the Engineering Students’ Society by becoming a member today! The Engineering Students’ Society works hard to enrich the engineering student experience for all engineering students by hosting a variety of academic, professional, and social events throughout the year. Members will enjoy perks such as discounted events, merchandise discounts, partner discounts, and early access to new ESS products and services.

Partner Benefits

The ESS works with businesses every year to provide you with some amazing discounts at numerous locations in Calgary. Here are some of the benefits you as an engineering student can get in the community with our membership:

Clear Float Spa

Clear Float Spa is an experience where you go into a sensory deprivation state by floating on water! They have agreed to provide 10% off to all Engineering Membership Cardholders.

Commonwealth

Commonwealth has not only offered to host events for us, but they have also agreed to provide no line/no cover charge benefits to everyone who holds an ESS membership card! (This only applies on non-special event days, and up until 11:00pm)

Eighty Eight Brewing Co

Eighty Eight Brewing Co has agreed to provide Engineering Membership Cardholders with 10% off all purchases made at their location. Their drinks are amazing.

Jameson's Pub

Jameson's Pub is a super close Irish themed pub to the University (only 20 min walk). They have agreed to provide all Engineering Membership Cardholders 15% off on all orders that aren't on special.

Rated Ultra Lounge

RatedUltraLounge has agreed to provide all Engineering Membership cardholders no line/no cover charges on non-special event days, and 10% off bottle service. Expect some events to be held here as well!

Red Bull

Huge thanks to Red Bull for providing us with some amazing drinks at our social events.

Red's Diner

ESS membership cardholders get 10% off all purchases made at the Kensington location. This is a great breakfast or brunch location.

The Village Flatbread Co

ESS members get 10% off all orders AND you can still get the discount if you order online with the promotional code #ESS. They have a variety of options including vegetarian and halal.


Conference and Event Opportunities

ESS members receive access to exclusive opportunities, which include:

  • Being able to apply for CFES and WEC engineering student conferences
  • Being able to participate in the Schulich Engineering Competition
  • Receiving discounts for the Engineering Dinner events happening in the winter semester

Additional Amenities & Discounts

ESS members receive access to the following services:

  • SIGNIFICANT DISCOUNTS ON ALL ESS MERCH INCLUDING PATCHES
  • Free printing of 10 double sided sheets of paper per day
  • Access to hot water
  • Charging cables (USB-C, lightning cable)
  • Calculator, lab coat, and projector free rental
