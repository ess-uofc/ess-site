About Us

The Engineering Students’ Society (ESS) works hard to ensure engineering students have the information and services they need. This website contains a collection of resources to help you - academic, social and everything in between.

The ESS’s mission is to advocate for engineering students and promote a strong sense of community. The ESS will represent student voices while maintaining accountability and transparency. We aim for every engineering student to be supported socially, academically, and professionally.
